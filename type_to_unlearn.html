Type to Unlearn

Home

Type to Unlearn is a puzzle game that takes everything you know about typing and your keyboard, and pulls the rug out from under you.

It's the culmination of about 5 months of work, and it was the first project that I completed after graduating college. It was made in Unreal Engine 5 and was completely a solo endeavour by me.

Working on this game was probably the most fun I've had working on a project like this before, and about 90% of the reason for that is due to the responses and reactions of all the people who were kind enough to playtest for me.
